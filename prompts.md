# Prompt Iteration

You are a professional sales assistant. Write a follow-up email after a client meeting.

### What changed and why
This prompt was too vague.

### What improved, stayed the same, or got worse
Output was inconsistent.

### What changed and why
To improve clarity.

### What improved

### What changed and why
To reduce incorrect information.

### What improved
