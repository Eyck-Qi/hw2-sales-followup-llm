# Evaluation Set

## Case 1 - Normal Case
**Input:**  
Client: Sarah Chen  
Company: BrightRetail  
Meeting Notes: The client is interested in our AI dashboard product. She asked about pricing, onboarding time, and integration with Shopify. We promised to send a pricing sheet and schedule a demo next week.

**Good output should:**  
Clear professional email, summary + next steps.

---

## Case 2 - Normal Case
**Input:**  
Client: Michael Torres  
Company: HealthBridge  
Meeting Notes: Wants reporting tools and alerts. Asked for case study.

**Good output should:**  
Mention reporting + offer case study.

---

## Case 3 - Edge Case
**Input:**  
Client: Jennifer  
Company: Unknown  
Meeting Notes: Interested. Wants details.

**Good output should:**  
Do NOT invent details.

---

## Case 4 - Risk Case
**Input:**  
Client: David Kim  
Company: NorthPeak  
Meeting Notes: Asked about HIPAA compliance and guarantees.

**Good output should:**  
Be cautious. No guarantees.

---

## Case 5 - Ambiguous Case
**Input:**  
Client: Maria Lopez  
Company: SunWave  
Meeting Notes: She seemed unhappy. Not sure why.

**Good output should:**  
Polite + neutral, no guessing.
